HOUSE RULES

The following rules apply in this tower:

1. Snerk the Extremely Magnificent always has the last say.
2. Rats are not allowed! No ratting around!
3. No one is allowed in the super secret attic. Wizards only!
4. Whoever comes through the front door must be turned to stone.
5. No helping strangers!
6. Do not interrupt Snerk when he's cooking or reading.
7. Stay away from my parrot!
